Adrian Zapletal

About me

I am a PhD student at the Delft University of Technology (TU Delft) under the supervision of Fernando Kuipers. I am part of the Networked Systems group at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Mathematics & Computer Science (EEMCS).

I received my Master's degree from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT) in 2022 and my Bachelor's degree from the University of Applied Sciences Stuttgart in 2017.

Research

My PhD is part of the CATRIN project, which aims to increase our society's trust in the Internet. I focus my research on fairness and control in the Internet.
