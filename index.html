Adrian Zapletal

About me

I am a PhD student at the Delft University of Technology (TU Delft) under the supervision of Fernando Kuipers. I am part of the Embedded and Networked Systems (ENS) group at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Mathematics & Computer Science (EEMCS).

I received my Master's degree from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT) in 2022 and my Bachelor's degree from the University of Applied Sciences Stuttgart in 2017.

Research

My research focuses on making the Internet more transparent, accountable, and controllable.

I also have an interest in the following topics: congestion control, Internet of Things (IoT).
